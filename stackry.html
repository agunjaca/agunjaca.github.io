Stackry brand refresh + web redesign Role: Lead Designer Year: 2022. Design Duration: 5 weeks
Stackry is a leading company in the Package Forwarding industry. By leveraging the latest technologies and relationships with the world's top international shippers, Stackry provides tools and pricing to make shopping in the USA as easy as shopping at home.
Stackry was established in 2012 and has grown to be a trusted package forwarder for over 100,000 international shoppers. The Stackry team has received over 2.5 million packages and shipped over 500,000 in that time.

Problems faced

- Bad UX - Outdated UI - Losing conversions due to bad user flows - Misleading buttons, titles and links - Bad customer satisfaction

Solutions conducted

- UX Overhaul - Multi- language support - Modern UI - Chatbot and very detailed FAQ - Intuitive shopping proccess
My Contribution: Everything (design-wise) from top do bottom since day one.
Rebrand of Stackry platform along with public website and user side (dashboard), including whole ordering process and everything that comes with it.
Last step was migrating over 120+ static pages used for SEO via new design template creation for developers to migrate old pages.
